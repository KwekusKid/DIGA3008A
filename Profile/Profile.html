APAYIN BOGOLO ODAME-TAKYI IS AN ARTIST OUT OF JOBURG WEST

I am an artist whose crafts include video game design, web design and photography. I'm currently studying at the University of the Witwatersrand, in my third year as BA in Digital Arts. I am majoring in Game Design and Interactive Media. I aim to tell stories across different medias, stories close to both my Tswana and Ghanian roots. The translation of my identity and my expressions hold the highest importance to me when it comes to going about any venture I pursue. I bring a strong foundation in creative direction, complemented by entry-level coding skills in C#, C++, JavaScript, and HTML. I am actively seeking opportunities in the field of art direction, where I can combine my creative vision with technical understanding to contribute to compelling visual and interactive experiences.
