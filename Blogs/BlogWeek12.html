A Decolonized Internet

By Apayin Odame - Takyi 2601677

Decolonialism and the progression of my website with javascript

What I want to change in my coding practice in the context of decolonialism?

Firstly, I want to get better at identifying coding practices that perpetuate colonialism. There's few I understand fully such as ignoring or not taking priority of local data. In this data driven society, I want to acknowledge local communities who will engage with my website and hopefully give them an enriching experience that is applicable to their context.

Secondly I want my website to have a deeper understanding of the context of my target audience and shape or form how can I enrich their context with my website. specifically in the long run, I would love to introduce a language feature specifically for different South African languages, where in which users on entry to my website can decide what language best suits their needs.
